⛓️ StoaChain Observation Pool (hub-fed reads) + Upload Pool (signed-tx senders).

Observation Pool

The hub-fed read fleet. Activate the AncientHub feed and reads fan out across the hub's StoaChain nodes (they earn PythXP). When the feed is off, reads use the Upload Pool.

Upload Pool

Signed-tx senders. Sends (/stoachain/send) go only here, in order (no PythXP), and also serve reads when the hub feed is off. The two seed nodes are baked in and can't be removed; add your own on top.
